The Real Impact?

Harmful content online often has a much bigger impact than you think. Beyond the people and groups targeted by the harmful content, both the readers and Indian society as a whole is negatively affected by the existence of this content. Here are a few reasons why:

Impact on You

Influences your thoughts and emotions, fueling anger, prejudice, and bias.

Reduces your ability to understand and empathize with diverse communities in India.

Negatively affects your overall well-being and relationships with people from different backgrounds.

Impact on Targeted Communities in India

Reinforces stereotypes, discrimination, and marginalization of specific groups within India.

Leads to social exclusion, harassment, and inequality for these targeted communities.

Can result in real-world violence and harm, exacerbating existing tensions.

Impact on Indian Society

Deepens divisions in Indian society and increases hostility between communities.

Undermines trust and cooperation among different groups within India.

Contributes to polarization, hindering dialogue and understanding necessary for social progress.
